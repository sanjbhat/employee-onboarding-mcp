SAW Device Request

Request your Secure Access Workstation (SAW) for accessing production systems and sensitive data.

What You Need to Do:

  1. Choose Your Device Type:
    • Laptop SAW: For remote work and travel (recommended for most roles)
    • Desktop SAW: For office-only work requiring high performance
    • Virtual SAW: For temporary access or specific use cases
  2. Get Manager Approval:
    • Contact your direct manager for approval
    • Your manager needs to provide their email for the request
    • Approval is typically processed within 1 business day
  3. Submit SAW Request:
    • Use the AI assistant's request_saw_device tool
    • Provide: device type and manager's email
    • You'll receive a request ID for tracking
  4. Wait for Processing:
    • IT Security will review your request (2-3 business days)
    • You'll receive email updates on the status
    • Device setup instructions will be sent upon approval
