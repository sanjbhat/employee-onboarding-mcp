Account Setup and Initial Access

Set up your basic company accounts and get initial access to core systems.

What You Need to Do:

  1. Company Portal Account:
    • Visit Company Portal
    • Use your corporate email to sign up
    • Complete the security setup (2FA required)
  2. Email Setup:
    • Configure Outlook with your corporate email
    • Download the Outlook app
    • Set up email forwarding if needed
  3. Teams Access:
    • Download Microsoft Teams
    • Join your department team
    • Update your profile picture and status
  4. Badge Photo:
    • Submit your photo via Badge Portal
    • Visit security desk for badge pickup (Building A, 1st floor)

Helpful Resources:

  • Complete Account Setup Guide
  • New Employee Orientation Video
  • IT Support - For technical issues
  • Security Policies and Guidelines

This step is complete when:

  • ✅ You can successfully log into the company portal
  • ✅ Your corporate email is working in Outlook
  • ✅ You can access Microsoft Teams
  • ✅ You have received your employee badge

Estimated Time: 2-3 hours
